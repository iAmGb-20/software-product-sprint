About Me

Godbless Chille

Univ. of Rochester '23

I am currently a sophomore at the University of Rochester majoring in Computer Science with a cluster in Religion and Psychology. I am mostly interested in Artificial Intelligence as well as Computer Systems. Aside of classes, I work as a Teaching Assistant for Data Structures and Algorithms and also as a Tour Guide for the Admissions and Financial Aid Office.

In my free time, you will either find me playing piano or playing video games. I am also a big fan of outdoor activities such as hiking and kayaking. Therefore, if you do not find me in the music room, or in my room, then you might be able to see me on Genesee River, trying to prevent myself from drowning with my kayak :)
